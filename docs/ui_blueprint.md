The Landing Page: This is the front door where players get their Anonymous ID and click "Start".

The Task Room: This is where the 10 reasoning questions live.

The Dashboard: This is where the player goes at the end to see charts and graphs of how they did.


(Navigation Flow)
1. Player arrives at the Landing Page.
2. Player clicks "Start"
3. Player sumbits an Answer
4. A Confidence Slider pops up(Scale 1-5)
5. Player gets Feedback(Correct/Incorrect)
6. Player clicks Next to go to the next question.
7. After 10 questions, player is sent to the Dashboard.

(Dashboard Layout)
A Pie Chart: Showing how many questions they got right vs wrong.
A Bar Chart: Showing their confidence(how sure they were) for each question.
A Timer Display: Showing how many seconds they spent on each riddle.

(Styling Guide)
Theme: White and Purple(Clean Laboratory look with purple accents)
Main Button Color: Purple
Success Color: Soft Green(Use this when a player gets an answer correct)
Incorrect Color: Soft Red(Use this when a player gets an answer wrong)
